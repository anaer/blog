### [whistle.inspect](https://github.com/whistle-plugins/whistle.inspect)

安装插件后, 设置并启用Rules规则, 刷新页面生效
支持: vconsole, eruda, mdebug

```conf
www.baidu.com whistle.inspect://eruda
```
